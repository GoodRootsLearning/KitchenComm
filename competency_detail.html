Competency: Exchange Safety Warnings

Goal: Correctly understand and use essential safety warnings (like "Hot," "Wet floor," "Sharp," "Careful") in English and Spanish during common kitchen situations.

Why it matters: Prevents burns, slips, cuts, and keeps everyone safe.

Mastery Requirement: You must successfully identify hazards and choose the correct warning in at least 4 out of 5 scenarios in the formal assessment simulation.
